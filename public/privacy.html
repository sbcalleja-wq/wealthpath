Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What We Collect

WealthPath collects information you voluntarily provide, including your age, investment amount, risk quiz answers, and chat messages with our AI assistant. If you connect a brokerage account through Plaid, we access read-only holdings data (tickers, quantities, values). We never access your brokerage login credentials — Plaid handles authentication directly.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to generate personalized portfolio recommendations and AI-powered analysis within the WealthPath application. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Brokerage Data (Plaid)

When you connect a brokerage account, Plaid Inc. securely handles the connection. WealthPath receives read-only access to your investment holdings. We cannot move money, place trades, or modify your accounts in any way. Plaid's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy at plaid.com/legal.

AI Chat Data

Messages you send to the AI assistant are processed through Anthropic's API to generate responses. These messages are not stored by Anthropic for training purposes. Your chat history may be saved in our database to restore your session when you return.

Data Storage

Session data (portfolio settings, chat history, profile information) may be stored in our database to provide continuity between visits. This data is associated with an anonymous session identifier, not your real identity, unless you voluntarily provide identifying information in the chat.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save your anonymous session ID so we can restore your portfolio when you return. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

Data Deletion

You can clear your session data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site. To request deletion of server-stored data, contact us at the email below.

Security

We use HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. Brokerage connections are handled by Plaid's SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

WealthPath is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at privacy@wealthpath.app.
